Minds in the end of the vacation
Hongjie Harry QIAN 钱鸿颉 Lv3
 2021-04-05 23:30:00  
  • Life 
 
  • Minds 
 

Actually it’s pretty busy these days, being around several places doing multiple things. I’m kinda exhausting. But more unfortunately, the following days in this semester might be more tiring.

It’s hard for be to have some time for scanning the memories of my passing-away families these days around the Qingming Festival, a festival for visiting their graves, expressing mixed minds for them and having an out for such wonderful spring. Struck in materials and works, I treasure the present 30 minutes for expressing my thoughts.

Cherry blossom in the campus everywhere cheers everyone in CPU, including me. As what it means in Japan, cherry blossom represents the extreme beauty of the grand time of someone’s life, and its fade can be extremely, as well, sad and tragic. It is nice that people can feel such strong emotions in a festival for memorize their passing relatives. In my minds, my grandpa, my grandma and my second aunt (all from my father’s side) are the most important people in such time.

EBV, malignant melanoma and lung cancer.

I still remember the tremendous sadness during their funerals and I think I could even touch it with my hands and eyes - such a complex emotion. Drowning in the non-stoppable tears, fainting could be possible and that truly happened.

No, I just want to stop here for the sadness. Cause that would be an endless story.


In such time, I just wanna feel their existence again with my imaginary.

I still hate the fate that they hadn’t taken an aeroplane for a single time. I love travelling in a long distance because that is fascinating and so expecting for me. “New trips, new sites, new people, new things…” I believe travelling for a long distance can mean a lot for many Chinese people like me because most of us have just got rid of poverty in the last approximately 40 years. Absence of such experience may mean not that much for living people, but hardly acceptable for them to see this happen on the passing people, including me.

Last National Day holidays I tried to plan a trip with my grandpa and I said it must be fun to have dim-sum in Guangzhou in the Spring Festival. However, everything happened fast.

On another aspect I think I can exactly feel the touch of my grandma and grandpa’s skin like holding their wrinkled skin. It’s certain that I must keep this in my mind till the end of my life.

I mean it.


Recently I am reading papers about ulcerative colitis for the iGEM CPU_CHINA, mainly about its immune physiopathogen and its mechanism related to ferroptosis (铁死亡). I couldn’t stop thinking if PD-1/PD-L1 could be used for the treatment of the malignant melanoma and the stage IV lung cancer of my second aunt. what could have happened?

I just can’t try to imagine, truly speaking.


I believe tomorrow is another day and I should fight for it with my efforts.

I will try to pursue the lights with my hands.

Next days more challenges are waiting for me and I have to stop here. Life is always running. I release these minds and am not expecting the feedback and only to place them in one place. Here they are.

Life is promising and I need to look straightforward with bravery.
